Do We Deserve It ?


Do We Deserve It ?


The sky, the Land ,the wind of course,
Are the nature's beauty in every sense.
The flowers that blossoms every spring,
The trees that shed in every autumn,
The brightful sky the twinkling stars,
The odourless air the tasteless water.
The nature has given everything,
but still a question raised in my mind,
Do we deserve it ?

The air to breath the water to drink,
The food to eat the trees to shelter,
The eyes to see the nose to breath,
The ear to hear the mouth to eat,
The tongue to speak the fingers to write,
The leg to stand the hand to do.
The nature has given everything,
but still a question raised in my mind,
Do we deserve it ?

The cloud to come the rain to fall,
The night to sleep the day to wake.
But still a question raised in my mind,
Do we deserve it ?
No no no we didn't deserve it ,
We are spoiling nature's art,
We make industries where forest to come,
We make land where water to come.
Don't think it will not punish ,
It will not pardon us for our every fault,
Earthquake come ,Tsunami comes,
And destroys our whole human world.
